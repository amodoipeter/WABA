What we do

We are a Kenyan High-Tech Water Company, With A Motivation To Build Sustainable Water Technologies For Developing countries.

Our signature Project "WABA" designs, develops and deploys self-operated water purification and vending machines
to enable communities in sub-Saharan Africa with an easy access to clean and safe drinking water at an affordable cost and in
a way which does not endanger our environment with plastic waste.

EXPENSIVE BOTTLED WATER

A liter of bottled water costs the same as a day’s water used in cooking, washing, showering, and other uses. Shockingly, bottled water is between 200 and 1,000 times more expensive than tap water. Comparatively, if a family paid bottled water prices for tap water, their monthly water bill would be close to $9,000!

ENVIRONMENTAL RISK

Thereare vatious risks but our aquatic life is the most affected when it comes to pollution by plastic materials.Over 1 million plastic bottles are purchased every minute and nearly 80% of these are washed up and end up in oceans and water masses.At this rate, the amount of single-use plastic ending up in our oceans will outweigh fish by 2050, unless some action is taken

HEALTH RISK

According to WHO, every minute a newborn dies from infection caused by lack of safe water and unclean environment. Contaminated drinking water is estimated to cause approximately 502,000 diarrhoeal deaths each year. Bisphenol A (BPA) and other chemicals used to make plastic bottles poses danger to those considering bottled water as an alternative.

The solution

WABA Water Is A Cost-Effective And Easy Alternative To Bottled Water, Providing Unlimited Access Filtered And Purified Water On The Go.

As Part Of Our Solution We Are Designing, Developing And Deploying Commercial
Grade And Sophisticated Water Purification And Vending Machines That Dispense Filtered And Pure Water in Various Areas In The Cities And Towns.

This Includes Completely Automated 9 Steps Of Purification.

We Have Created A Responsible Production And Consumption
Chain That Substantially Reduces Waste Generation Through Preventing, Reducing, Recycling And Reusing.
With Our Cost Effective Model, We Ensure Safe Water, Good Health And Well Being For All.

Our Impact Includes Reduced Number Of Deaths And Illnesses From Water Contamination,
Higher College Completion Rates, Single Use Bottle Avoidance, CO2 Mitigation And Equitable Access To Safe And Affordable Drinking Water!
